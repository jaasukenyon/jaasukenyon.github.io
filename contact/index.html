If you’re interested in working together—or just want to ask a few questions—I’d be happy to hear from you.

Whether you’re a student, parent, or adult learner, I offer a free consultation to discuss your goals and determine how I can help.

📬 Reach Out

  • Email: jaasukenyon@protonmail.com
  • Subject Line: “Tutoring Inquiry”
  • Please include:
    • Your name (or your child’s name and grade level)
    • The subject(s) you’re seeking help with
    • Your availability for a free 15–30 minute consultation

🤝 What to Expect

Our first interaction is meant to be low-pressure and informative. Here’s what happens next:

  1. Initial Consultation (free)
    We’ll discuss your learning goals, current challenges, and what you’re hoping to get out of tutoring. I’ll ask some questions to better understand your needs.

  2. Diagnostic Review (if needed)
    I may recommend a short diagnostic session or review of prior work to assess foundational gaps and plan our approach.

  3. Plan and Scheduling
    If it’s a good fit, we’ll agree on a schedule and define clear goals for your first month of sessions. All sessions are customized to your pace and learning style.

💡 Not Sure Yet?

That’s perfectly fine.

Even if you’re unsure whether tutoring is the right fit, you’re welcome to reach out with questions. I’m happy to help you think through options or make a plan—even if you decide not to continue.

🎯 Serious learning takes clarity, structure, and a bit of courage. I’ll help you bring all three.

Looking forward to hearing from you.
